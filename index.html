About Us

Playful Minds Services provides professional early childhood and family day care support, focused on creating safe, nurturing, and engaging environments for children.

Our approach values curiosity, relationships, and meaningful learning experiences that support children from birth through the early years.

Our Services

  • Early childhood education support
  • Family Day Care assistance and guidance
  • Programming and documentation support
  • Child-centred, play-based learning approaches

Commitment to Quality & Safety

We operate within relevant regulatory and quality frameworks and are committed to respectful, ethical, and confidential practices.

Children’s wellbeing, safety, and dignity are always our priority.
